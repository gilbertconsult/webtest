    About

    The Twin Cities InDesign User Group is a loose affiliation of about 400 designers, production artists, editors, book builders, instructors, students, and others who are passionate about taking their InDesign skills to the next level.

    We have been meeting regularly since 2004. The user group has grown into a community during this time whose members value each others input.

    Meetings are organized and sponsored by Keith Gilbert of Gilbert Consulting and Ed Klemz of Central Coast Solutions.
