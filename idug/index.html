    ﻿About

    The Twin Cities InDesign User Group is a loose affiliation of about 400 designers, production artists, editors, book builders, instructors, students, and others who are passionate about taking their InDesign skills to the next level.

    We have been meeting regularly since 2004. The user group has grown into a community whose members value each others input.

    Each meeting typically consists of one or more presentations, each with question and answer time, followed by a raffle for door prizes. Presentations are by industry experts as well as users who are willing to share their knowledge and hard-won experience with others.

    Meetings free and open to the public thanks to our sponsors Keith Gilbert of Gilbert Consulting and Ed Klemz of Central Coast Solutions.
